My Life Before LaunchCode

College!

I went to Mizzou for a year between 2012 and 2013. I was a Film Studies Major.

During my time there I started performing stand up and sketch comedy.

I realized I wasn't happy with my major choice and through a "mutual" decision with the school I decided to leave

Post Drop-Out!

After moving back home, I started doing more comedy and even made friends!

Focusing On Comedy!

Over the course of 4 years I got to travel the country and meet some of my heroes and meet funny comics from all acros the country!

I even had the opprotunity to open for Wyatt Cenac once!

But Something Was Missing!

Despite my newfound happiness, I was still working a part time job and wasn't making enough money to do comedy full time.'

I needed to make a change in my life for the better. That was when I heard about LaunchCode.

I decided to sign up and that's kind of where the story ends. There's more details but that's not important right now.
